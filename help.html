What commands are included with tr&chat?

Here is a nice little reference you can use (it will be updated as more commands are implemented):

  • /roll /rawl /jimmyrollins
    Arguably the most important and holy command, /roll /rolls a number given to us by the QUADSGODS and the Ancients. If you get repeating digits you have a large penis/breasts. You will also be lauded by various figures of great internet importance like Patrick Bateman. As an added bonus, people will tell tales about your >>88888888 GET to their children.
  • /clear /c
    Clears the chat immediately on your end. Good for spam. Also, the X on the topbar does the same function.(Daily reminder: you can disable 'Maximum Maymay Mode' in the options if those memes get out of hand.)
  • /yt{paste url of youtube video}
    Embeds a youtube video into the chat, given a valid URL. Just copy the URL and type in /yt and then paste. There is also a GUI version in the 'share' window, but this is quick and easy.
  • /web /www{paste url of website}
    Embeds a website into the chat. Some websites will not allow this feature however. Good for posting threads on reddit or 8chan, and maybe sharing some news directly in the chat! (Also a GUI version in 'Share' window if you wish.)
  • /polska /sciernisko (or just type in 'kurwa')
    Play a hideously low bitrate compressed version of the 'Polish National Anthem' for our fellow bracia in mother poland. (You can use /c /clear or the clear button (X) on the top bar to be a filthy american.

How do I call people in tr&chat?

In the topbar, click the phone icon, and a panel will slide down. This is the call panel. To begin a conference, simply click Host Call (or whatever I might rename it to). If someone has already started a call, all you have to do is open the call panel and you should join automagically! To leave the call, press the leave call button duh.

Wait... how do I mute myself?

When you connect to the call, you'll notice you have your own little box, referred to henceforth as your stream. You can pause/click your stream to effectively stop people from receiving your stream, hence muting yourself.

How do I change how tr&chat looks like?

Click the cog icon to open up the Options Panel

  1. Select any of the premade styles in the Options Panel.
  2. Paste your own stylesheet into the text area and then press the RICE! button to rice it up!
  3. Edit the CSS rules in your browser.
