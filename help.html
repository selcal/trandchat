What commands are included with tr&chat?

  • /yt{paste url of youtube video}
    Embeds a youtube video into the chat, given a valid URL. Just copy the URL and type in /yt and then paste. There is also a GUI version in the 'share' window, but this is quick and easy.
  • /web /www{paste url of website}
    Embeds a website into the chat. Some websites will not allow this feature. Good for posting threads on reddit, and maybe sharing some news directly in the chat!
  • /clear /c
    Clears the chat immediately on your end. Good for spam. The X on the topbar does the same thing.
  • /pic /img{paste url of image}
    Places an image from the web directly into the chat.

How do I change how tr&chat looks like?

Click the settings icon in the top right to open up the Options Panel

  1. Select a preset background (soon you'll be able to upload your own)
  2. Select any of the premade styles in the Options Panel.
  3. Edit the CSS rules in your browser.
